Personality

Fluffy, a true gentleman, lives up to his name with a coat as soft as a cloud. This charming British Shorthair is a cuddle champion, always happy to receive chin scratches and head boops. Despite his love for affection, Fluffy maintains an independent spirit. He enjoys solo playtime with feathery toys and exploring sunny spots for naps. His captivating yellow eyes will hold your gaze, and his gentle purr is the perfect soundtrack to a relaxing evening.


Ideal Home

Fluffy would thrive in a loving home that appreciates his playful yet laid-back personality. He'd do well with someone who can provide him with regular playtime and opportunities to show off his hunting prowess with interactive toys. An environment with scratching posts and cat trees would be ideal for him to indulge in his natural instincts.

Adoption Rules
General Requirements
  • Be 18 years or older
  • Valid government ID
  • Proof of address (utility bill, lease agreement)
  • Landlord approval if renting (if applicable)
Be a Responsible and Loving Guardian

This means being able to provide for the cat's basic needs (food, water, litter box, shelter), offering them veterinary care, and showering them with love and attention.
If you can confidently say you can offer a stable and caring environment, you're on the right track to adopting a cat!
